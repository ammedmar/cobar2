\begin{abstract}
	We provide the extended cobar construction on the chains of a reduced simplicial set with the structure of a monoidal $E_\infty$-coalgebra, and connect it through a natural zig-zag of quasi-isomorphisms to the chains on the Kan loop group regarded as an $E_\infty$-coalgebra.
\end{abstract}
